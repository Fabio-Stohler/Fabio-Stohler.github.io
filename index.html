Fabio Stohler

Ph.D. candidate in Economics

Welcome to my website! I am a Ph.D. candidate in economics at the University of Bonn, where I focus on macroeconomics and monetary theory. In addition to these research interests, I am also interested in practical monetary policy, inequality, and climate change.

Prior to pursuing my Ph.D., I completed studies in Business Administration in Lörrach and Cardiff, and Econmics in Lisbon and São Paulo. In addition to my academic pursuits, I have work experience in the private sector, and in teaching.

If you want to work with me or simply reach out, feel free to contact me via email.
